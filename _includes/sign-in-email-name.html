An email address as a piece of data is usually unique, unlike a name. We use them to tell the difference between one "John Smith" and another.

   
 

How do we use this data?

1: To create aggregated reports for our funders and community on the reach of the ODI, this helps us secure new funding and keep going.

2: To enable us to audit the attendance of paid ODI events and training and provide a legal proof of fulfilment of contract.

If your visit is not related to a paid event or training and you feel that your own legitimate interest outweighs that outlined in (1) then you can choose not to sign-in.

We do not share the raw data with anyone outside the ODI.
